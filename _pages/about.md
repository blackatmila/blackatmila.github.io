Welcome to Black at Mila, a community dedicated to supporting and celebrating Black researchers, students, and professionals at [Mila - Quebec AI Institute](https://mila.quebec/). We create spaces for sharing ideas, fostering collaborations, mentorship and advocacy.

Our Mission
======
Black at Mila serves as a platform to amplify the voices, research, and contributions of Black members within the Mila community. We are committed to:

- **Building Community**: Creating a supportive network for Black researchers, students, and professionals at Mila
- **Showcasing Excellence**: Highlighting the groundbreaking work and achievements of Black members
- **Promoting Diversity**: Advocating for greater representation and inclusion in AI research
- **Sharing Knowledge**: Facilitating the exchange of ideas through talks, publications, and collaborative projects

Stay Connected
======
Follow our activities and connect with the Black at Mila community. We regularly share updates about research, events, and opportunities that support our mission of fostering an inclusive and diverse AI research environment.
